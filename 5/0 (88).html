89. A Paper Clip




     089 "

Jim had two separate sheets of paper. He wanted to put the two sheets of paper together. He d not want to glue them together. He d not want to use glue. He d not want to staple them together. He d not want to use a staple. What d Jim want to use? He wanted to use a paper clip. He wanted to put the two sheets of paper together with a paper clip. He picked up a paper clip. It was a metal paper clip. It was a silver paper clip. Jim put the silver metal paper clip on both sheets of paper. Now the sheets of paper were together. They were not separate.
